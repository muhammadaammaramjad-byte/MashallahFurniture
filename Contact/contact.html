Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused items in their original packaging. Custom orders and clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned. Returns are subject to a restocking fee of 15%.

Do you offer custom furniture design?

Yes, we have an in-house design team that can create custom furniture pieces tailored to your specific needs and space. Custom orders require a 50% deposit and have a lead time of 6-8 weeks.

What are your delivery options?

We offer standard delivery (5-7 business days) for $49, express delivery (2-3 business days) for $99, and white-glove delivery service for large items which includes assembly and placement in your home for $199.

Do you provide assembly services?

Yes, we offer professional assembly services for an additional fee. Our team will assemble your furniture at your home and ensure everything is properly set up. Assembly services start at $79 depending on the complexity of the item.

What materials do you use for your furniture?

We use only premium materials including solid hardwoods (oak, walnut, and maple), high-quality veneers, tempered glass, and premium upholstery fabrics from leading manufacturers. All our finishes are non-toxic and environmentally friendly.
