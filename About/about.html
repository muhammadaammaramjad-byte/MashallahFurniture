At Ramma Furniture, we blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to create pieces that stand the test of time. Each item is meticulously crafted with attention to detail and a passion for excellence.

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2005, Ramma Furniture started as a small workshop with a big vision: to create beautiful, functional furniture that enhances everyday living. What began as a passion project has grown into a respected name in the furniture industry.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovative design. We source only the finest materials and employ skilled artisans who take pride in their craft.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while embracing new techniques and technologies that allow us to push the boundaries of furniture design.

Our Values

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices, from sourcing sustainable materials to minimizing waste in our production process.

Craftsmanship

Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their work, ensuring exceptional quality and attention to detail.

Innovation

We continuously explore new designs, materials, and techniques to create furniture that is both functional and aesthetically groundbreaking.

Our Journey

2005

Founded as a small workshop with just three craftsmen and a vision to create beautiful, functional furniture.

2010

Opened our first showroom and introduced the iconic Rama Collection, which remains a bestseller to this day.

2015

Achieved international recognition with awards for design excellence and sustainable manufacturing practices.

2020

Launched our e-commerce platform, making our furniture accessible to customers worldwide during the pandemic.

2023

Expanded our product line to include smart furniture with integrated technology while maintaining our commitment to craftsmanship.
